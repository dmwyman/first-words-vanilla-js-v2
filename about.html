First Words

Purpose

First Words is designed to help children and other language learners in the early stages of learning by gaining fluency with basic English words. We hope to make the learning experience fun and enjoyable.

Creator

David Wyman, the creator of First Words, is a student of several languages, ancient and modern. He writes academic articles in the fields of papyrology and New Testament. He is currently working on his first book, a revised version of his 2022 PhD dissertation.
